Eye Movement Exercise

In this project, as one runs the program, two eyes will appear in the screen and move according to the movement of the mouse. Demonstration of how Javascript can be used to work with computer IO (input/outputs). In the project, the mouse movement is tracked and other elements (eyes) are moved accordingly.
